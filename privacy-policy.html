Privacy Policy

Vaporwave & Synthwave Music

Last updated: December 2024

Quick Summary: We do not collect, store, or share any personal information from users of the Vaporwave & Synthwave Music app. Your privacy is completely protected.

1. Information We Collect

We do not collect any personal information. The Vaporwave & Synthwave Music app is designed to function without requiring or collecting any personal data from our users, including but not limited to:

  • Names, email addresses, or contact information
  • Location data
  • Device identifiers
  • Usage patterns or listening history
  • Account information (no account creation required)

2. App Functionality

Our app provides access to various radio stations streaming synthwave, vaporwave, and related music genres. The app functions entirely without requiring user registration or login credentials. All features are available immediately upon installation.

3. Advertising

This app is free to download and use, and we support it through advertising. Our advertising partners may display ads within the app, but we do not share any personal information with them because we don't collect any personal information to begin with.

The ads you see may be:

  • Generic ads not based on personal data
  • Contextual ads related to music or entertainment
  • Ads served by third-party advertising networks

4. Third-Party Services

The app streams music from various radio stations that are operated by third parties. We do not control the privacy practices of these radio stations. We recommend reviewing the privacy policies of individual radio stations if you have concerns about their data practices.

5. Data Storage

The app may store minimal technical data locally on your device such as:

  • Your preferred radio stations (favorites)
  • App settings and preferences
  • Recorded music files (if you use the recording feature)

This information remains on your device and is not transmitted to our servers or shared with third parties.

6. Children's Privacy

Our app does not collect personal information from anyone, including children under 13. The app is suitable for all ages and does not require parental supervision regarding data collection since no data is collected.

7. Data Security

Since we do not collect personal information, there is no personal data at risk. Any local data stored on your device is protected by your device's security measures.

8. Your Rights
