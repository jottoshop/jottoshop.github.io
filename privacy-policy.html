Privacy Policy

Vaporwave & Synthwave Music

Last updated: May 2025

Quick Summary: Our app does not directly collect personal information, but we use third-party services (Firebase Analytics and Google AdMob) that may collect certain data for analytics and advertising purposes. Your data is handled according to Google's privacy policies.

1. Information We Collect

We do not directly collect personal information. However, our app uses third-party services that may collect certain data as described below:

Firebase Analytics

We use Firebase Analytics to understand how users interact with our app. Firebase may collect:

  • App Activity: App interactions, page views, button clicks
  • App Info and Performance: Crash logs and diagnostics
  • Device Information: Device model, operating system, app version

Google AdMob

We use Google AdMob to display advertisements. AdMob may collect:

  • Device or Other IDs: Advertising ID, Android ID
  • App Activity: Installed apps (for ad targeting)
  • App Info and Performance: Crash logs and diagnostics
  • Device Information: Device specifications for ad compatibility

We do not collect:

  • Names, email addresses, or contact information
  • Precise location data
  • Audio recordings or music listening history
  • Account information (no account creation required)

2. App Functionality

Our app provides access to various radio stations streaming synthwave, vaporwave, and related music genres. The app functions entirely without requiring user registration or login credentials. All features are available immediately upon installation.

3. How We Use Information

The data collected by our third-party services is used to:

  • Analytics: Understand app usage patterns and improve user experience
  • Performance: Monitor app performance and fix crashes
  • Advertising: Display relevant ads and measure ad performance

4. Data Sharing

We share data with the following third-party services:

  • Google AdMob: For advertising services and ad personalization
  • Firebase/Google Analytics: For app analytics and performance monitoring
  • Third-party advertising partners: AdMob may share data with ad networks for targeted advertising

Important: These third-party services have their own privacy policies that govern how they handle your data. We recommend reviewing:

  • Google Privacy Policy
  • AdMob Privacy Policy
  • Firebase Privacy Policy

5. Advertising

This app is free to download and use, and we support it through advertising via Google AdMob. AdMob may use your Advertising ID and other data to:

  • Display personalized ads based on your interests
  • Measure ad performance and engagement
  • Share data with advertising partners for ad targeting

Ad Personalization Control: You can opt out of personalized ads by:

  • Going to your device's Settings → Google → Ads
  • Turning on "Opt out of Ads Personalization"

6. Third-Party Radio Stations

The app streams music from various radio stations that are operated by third parties. We do not control the privacy practices of these radio stations. We recommend reviewing the privacy policies of individual radio stations if you have concerns about their data practices.

7. Data Storage and Retention

The app may store minimal data locally on your device such as:

  • Your preferred radio stations (favorites)
  • App settings and preferences
  • Recorded music files (if you use the recording feature)

This local information remains on your device and is not transmitted to our servers. Data collected by Firebase and AdMob is retained according to their respective privacy policies.

8. Children's Privacy

Our app is suitable for all ages. We do not knowingly collect personal information from children under 13 through our app directly. However, Firebase and AdMob may collect certain technical data as described in their privacy policies. If you are a parent and believe your child has provided personal information, please contact us.

9. Data Security

We rely on Google's security infrastructure for Firebase and AdMob services. Any local data stored on your device is protected by your device's security measures. We do not store personal data on our own servers.

10. Your Rights and Choices

You have the following rights regarding your data:

  • Opt-out of personalized ads: Through your device's ad settings
  • Reset Advertising ID: Through your device's privacy settings
  • Uninstall the app: To stop all data collection related to our app
  • Contact Google: For data requests related to Firebase/AdMob data

We may update this privacy policy from time to time. Any changes will be posted here and will take effect immediately upon posting. We will also update the "Last updated" date at the top of this policy.
